Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep Time:15 minutes

Cook Time:10 minutes

Steps
  1. preheat the oven to 350 oF(180oC)
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add choclate chips and stir well
  4. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden browns
